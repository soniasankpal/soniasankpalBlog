Poems


सांजवेळ

As there are ups and downs in everyone's life. Someone can lose his/her hope in such situations. But there is a ray of Hope always. One can extract his/her hope by seeking around the nature of "OCEAN".

आयुष्य जगणं म्हणजे

Expect the best. Prepare for the worst. Capitalise on what comes. Everyone has their separate road to travel their lives and this one is mine.

तलाश

Human is a greedy animal. His thrust for achievements never goes down. Someone want money, relation, goal or someone is just seeking for peace of mind. As every cloud has silver lining and the man don't stop until he gets his silver lining तबतक तलाश जारी है.

अपूर्ण कादंबरी

True Love means to feel someone in every heartbeat, to find someone in every thought .But sometimes the love story never completes it remained as INCOMPLETE.

रम्य जीवन

Love is fragile. One must live in the magic of love and feel its intensity. If one finds the true Love then he must understand he is with his shadow.

मी वाट पाहीन

Why it is so hard and everything's so complicated in his mind. He was waiting and is waiting and will be waiting for his love.

रितेपण

Sometimes life shows us the truth we never expected to face. This truth always pinch the person and make him realise that no matter how many people he was surrounded with , he came in this world alone, has to live alone and has to leave alone.

1 BHK

Relations are meant to be preserved. It is a scenario that the more one get civilised the more they loosen their relations. Here a man expresses his feeling of being apart from his HOME, seeking for 1 BHK.

अश्वत्थामा

Aswatthama one of the most important person in epic Mahabharata. He is the lone survivor still living, who actually fought in the Kurukshetra. Here is what he might be feeling after thousands of years.

या वाटेवरुन

No one saves us but ourselves. No one can and no one may. We ourselves must walk the path .It always seems impossible until it is done. So just walk on the path and turn impossible in to possible.
